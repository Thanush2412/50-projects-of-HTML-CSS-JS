How It Works

This app uses the Web Audio API to record and play back your voice notes.

  • MediaRecorder API: Records audio from your microphone
  • AudioContext: Processes audio data and creates visualizations
  • localStorage: Saves your recordings between sessions

Your recordings are stored locally on your device and are not sent to any server.
